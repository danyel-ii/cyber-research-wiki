# Penetration Testing

Penetration testing is the top-level entry point for this wiki. Use it for articles that explain what penetration testing is for, how it differs from adjacent practices, what kinds of questions a pentest answers, and how owners should think about validated security exposure.

## What belongs here
- articles about the purpose and scope of penetration testing
- comparisons with adjacent practices such as assessment or red teaming
- articles on validated exposure, evidence, and reporting value
- broad methodology pieces that are not tied to one narrow domain

## How this category is positioned
This is the front door of the eight-category map. Readers should normally begin here, then move into workflow, rules of engagement, environment, and domain-specific branches.
