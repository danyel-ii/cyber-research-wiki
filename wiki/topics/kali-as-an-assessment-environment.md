# Kali as an Assessment Environment

This category exists to enforce an important distinction: Kali Linux is an assessment environment, not a methodology. Use it for articles that explain why that distinction matters and how environment design supports, but does not define, authorized security work.

## What belongs here
- environment-versus-methodology articles
- portability and repeatability as environment traits
- operator-readiness discussions
- articles that generalize from Kali to broader environment thinking

## How this category is positioned
This is the conceptual branch of the Kali cluster. It keeps the wiki from collapsing environment choice into security methodology.
