# Recon

Recon is the discovery and orientation layer of the wiki. Use it for material that helps the reader understand what exists, how a target is exposed, where boundaries sit, and what deserves deeper scrutiny before intrusive validation starts.

## How to use this category
Start here when the problem is target understanding rather than payload delivery. Recon should turn an unknown environment into a legible one.

## What belongs here
- asset discovery, enumeration, and attack-surface mapping
- service and application discovery
- internet-facing exposure analysis
- environment and lab decisions that directly support early assessment work
