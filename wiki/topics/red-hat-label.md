# Red Hat (Label)

## Overview
This page documents the informal `red hat` label as a term that may appear in popular explanations of hacker categories.

## Why this wiki does not use it as a core category
In professional security practice, `red team` is the more standard term for structured adversary-emulation work. `Red hat` is less standardized and may be defined inconsistently across sources.

## How to use this page
Capture:
- definitions from sources that use the term
- ambiguities and competing meanings
- whether a source really means `red team` instead
