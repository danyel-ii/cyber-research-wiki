# Authorized Security Research

## Overview
This page defines the legal and ethical frame for the wiki. The default stance is that testing and research discussed here should be authorized, bounded, and documented.

## Core idea
Authorized research is not merely about technical skill. It also depends on scope, permission, rules of engagement, reporting, and clear defensive purpose.

## Why this matters
- It separates legitimate assessment from unauthorized intrusion.
- It determines what methods are permissible in practice.
- It affects how findings should be documented and escalated.
