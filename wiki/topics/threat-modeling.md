# Threat Modeling

## Overview
Threat modeling is a structured way to reason about likely threats, attack paths, assumptions, and defenses before or alongside hands-on testing.

## Why it matters here
A good research wiki should connect testing activity to priorities. Threat modeling helps explain why a test matters, what should be emulated, and which controls deserve attention.
