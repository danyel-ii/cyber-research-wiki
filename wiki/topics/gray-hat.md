# Gray Hat

## Overview
`Gray hat` (also spelled `grey hat`) is an informal term for activity that is often described as sitting between clearly authorized security work and clearly malicious activity.

## Why this is not a primary organizing concept here
The term is memorable, but it is often too vague for rigorous research organization. This wiki prefers concrete dimensions such as authorization status, methodology, target domain, and evidence quality.

## How to use this page
Use this page to collect:
- differing definitions from sources
- legal and ethical tensions
- case studies where the label is applied
- reasons the label may obscure more than it clarifies

## Tensions / contradictions
- Some sources frame gray-hat activity as benevolent but unauthorized.
- Others use the term loosely for any activity in a legal or ethical gray area.
