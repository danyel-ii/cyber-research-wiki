# AGENTS.md for wiki/

## Purpose
`wiki/` is the maintained knowledge layer.

## Mandatory conventions
- Every substantive page must have a concise `## Overview` section.
- Factual pages should include `## Sources` or `## Evidence and sources`.
- Pages that contain uncertainty should include `## Open questions` and/or `## Tensions / contradictions`.
- Use internal links generously, but only when they are meaningful.
- Keep sections scannable. Prefer small sections over long walls of text.

## Required special files
- `index.md` is the catalog. Update it whenever pages are created, renamed, or materially reframed.
- `log.md` is append-only. Add a dated entry for ingests, saved analyses, and lint passes.

## Naming
- Use kebab-case filenames.
- Start dated analysis pages with `YYYY-MM-DD-`.
- Prefer stable semantic names over source-specific names for topic pages.
