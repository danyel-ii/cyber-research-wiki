# Lint Wiki

Use this skill when asked to review the health of the wiki.

## Checks
- orphan pages
- duplicate or near-duplicate pages
- weak or missing internal links
- stale claims superseded by newer material
- recurring concepts without dedicated pages
- pages with assertions but thin provenance
- open questions that now look answerable

## Editing policy
Prefer small corrective edits over structural churn.

## Definition of done
- make obvious small fixes directly
- summarize larger issues as recommendations
- append a lint entry to `wiki/log.md`
